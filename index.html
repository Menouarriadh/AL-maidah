A Table United
by Heritage

Born from a passion for authentic Mediterranean flavors, Al Maidah — meaning "The Table" in Arabic — was founded to celebrate the rich culinary traditions of Algeria, Tunisia, and Syria under one roof.

Every dish is crafted from time-honored family recipes, fragrant spices sourced directly from the Maghreb and Levant, and locally fresh ingredients. We cook with memory and with love.
